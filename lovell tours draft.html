Lovell Health House Tours

90th Anniversary!

Join architectural preservationist and current Health House resident Joshua Gorrell, historian and author Lyra Kilston, and 40-year Health House owner Ken Topper for a special tour of this iconic modernist masterpiece by Richard Neutra, exactly 90 years after it was completed in December 1929. Hear the fascinating stories behind the house's groundbreaking design and construction, its famed architect and eccentric client--naturopathic doctor Philip Lovell--and the health crazes that swept through Los Angeles in the early 20th century.
